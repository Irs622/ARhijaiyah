# Research Gap Analysis

## Gap 1: Lack of Sequential Persistent Rendering

Most AR applications use one-to-one marker mapping where previously rendered objects disappear when new markers are detected.

Proposed Solution:
Persistent rendering mechanism that preserves previously scanned Hijaiyah letters.

---

## Gap 2: Limited Multi-Target Performance Evaluation

Few studies evaluate memory usage and rendering performance under multiple simultaneous AR objects.

Proposed Solution:
Benchmark FPS, memory usage, and rendering stability.

---

## Gap 3: Lack of Pronunciation Evaluation

Most educational AR systems focus only on visualization.

Proposed Solution:
Speech recognition based pronunciation assessment.

---

## Gap 4: Limited Quantitative Usability Metrics

Few studies investigate how persistent AR affects cognitive retention.

Proposed Solution:
SUS and UEQ evaluation.
